I would like to have a good computer that fits different needs.

**For studies**, I need a laptop with a fast processor and at least 8 GB of RAM. It should have a comfortable keyboard and a screen of about 14 inches. I will use it for writing essays, searching information online, and watching educational videos. The most useful applications for me are Microsoft Word, Google Chrome, and Zoom for online classes.

**For work**, I think a more powerful configuration is better. I would like a computer with 16 GB of RAM and a strong processor to run many programs at the same time. A larger screen (15–16 inches) is also helpful. Useful applications include Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and project‑management tools like Trello. If I do creative work, I might need Adobe Photoshop or Illustrator.

**For entertainment**, I want a computer with a good graphics card and a high‑resolution screen. This is important for playing modern video games and watching movies in high quality. I will also use YouTube, Spotify, and streaming platforms like Netflix. A comfortable mouse and good headphones make entertainment even better.
